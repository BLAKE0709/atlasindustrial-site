The Model

Thermal Symbiosis

A closed-loop system where industrial facilities and compute infrastructure become mutually reinforcing — waste from one is fuel for the other.

1

Capture

Harvest thermal energy that industrial processes currently vent to the atmosphere as waste.

2

Convert

Transform captured heat into electricity using proven thermodynamic conversion technology.

3

Power

Direct all generated electricity to on-site AI and cloud compute infrastructure.

4

Return

Route residual heat from compute cooling back to the industrial partner, closing the loop.

About

Built for the Long Term

Atlas Industrial is a compute infrastructure company headquartered in Texas. We develop and operate behind-the-meter microgrids that convert industrial waste energy into compute capacity.

Our model eliminates the two biggest constraints facing new compute deployment: power availability and permitting timelines. By co-locating with existing industrial operations, we bring compute online faster, cheaper, and cleaner.

We are building Atlas Industrial as durable infrastructure — the kind of company that creates compounding value over decades, not quarters.
